# Writeup

This project's findings are published as two blog posts:

1. **[Yes, LLMs Can Be Computers. Now What?](https://muninn.austegard.com/blog/yes-llms-can-be-computers-now-what)** — Validating Percepta's claims from first principles: 13 phases, a productive wrong turn through training, and a working compiled transformer executor.

2. **[The Free Computer: Why Offloading to CPU Is a Win for Everyone](https://muninn.austegard.com/blog/the-free-computer-why-offloading-to-cpu-is-a-win-for-everyone)** — The economic argument: compiled execution on CPU frees GPU cycles for what only GPUs can do.
